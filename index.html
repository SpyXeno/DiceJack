WHAT IS DICEJACK?

DiceJack is a blend of BlackJack and dice rolls where your goal is to win a turn based battle by rolling multiple dice one after the other trying to get as close as possible to your Bust limit before attacking, but if you Bust, you will do 0 damage for the turn.
The goal is to defeat 5 different Enemy classes of Heroes in the Gauntlet and ultimately beat the Boss Fight to win the game.
There are 10+ classes of heroes to choose from so find what suits your playstyle and learn the strengths and weaknesses of each to beat the game.
Read more on how a Battle works on the next page.

HOW TO BATTLE

Each side has 30HP in a battle (45 for Boss), whoever loses all of their HP first loses the battle. After winning a Battle you will move on to battle the next class until you reach the Boss.
Initiative Roll
Before every Battle an Initiative Roll of three dice is played on both sides, the person who rolled the highest value gets the first turn in Battle. A tie results in a re-roll until one side wins.
Roll
This action will roll a dice, adding to your total displayed in the center of the screen, you can roll as long as you have rolls left or until you Bust.
Attack
Once you are satisfied with the current roll value, you will choose to Attack which will damage the enemy for the amount you rolled.
Bust
If you roll a value that exceeds your max limit, you will Bust which makes you lose the turn and do 0 damage. Every class varies in the amount of dice they can roll and the max bust value so play to your classes strengths.
Skill
Every class has their own unique skills which can be activated during a turn, these can be effectively used to turn the tides of a battle and even counter enemy skills.
Healing
Rolling the same value of dice twice in a row heals for 10HP.
